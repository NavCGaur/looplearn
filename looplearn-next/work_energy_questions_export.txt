# Work and Energy (Class 9) Questions

Q1: What is work done when a force of 7 N displaces an object by 8 m in the direction of force?
A) 15 J
B) 56 J
C) 1 J
D) 0 J

Q2: When do we say work is done?
A) Only when force acts on object
B) Only when object moves
C) When force acts AND causes displacement
D) When object changes shape

A) Work done by 1 N force over 1 m
B) Work done by 2 N force over 1 m
C) Work done by 1 N force over 2 m
D) Force of 1 N acting for 1 second

Q4: What is the SI unit of work?
Answer: __________

Q5: What is kinetic energy?
A) Energy due to position
B) Energy due to motion
C) Energy due to heat
D) Energy stored in springs

Q6: What is the formula for kinetic energy?

B) ½mv²
C) mv²
D) mgh

A) 2 times
B) 4 times
C) 8 times
D) Remains same

A) 3 times
B) 6 times
C) 9 times
D) 27 times

Q9: What is power?
A) Total work done
B) Rate of doing work
C) Force applied
D) Energy stored

Q10: What is the SI unit of power?
Answer: __________

Q11: Define 1 watt of power.
A) 1 J of work in 1 s
B) 2 J of work in 1 s

D) 1 N of force

B) 100 W
C) 1000 W
D) 10000 W

Q13: A person swimming in a pond is doing work because:
A) Force causes displacement
B) Getting tired means work
C) Moving in water always is work

B) No, force perpendicular to displacement
