# Force and Laws of Motion (Class 9) Questions

Q1: Newton's ___ law states that every action has an equal and opposite reaction
Answer: __________

D) Cannot be determined

B) Mass
C) Shape
D) Color

B) Ground pushes horse forward in reaction to horse pushing ground back

Q9: Why is it difficult for a fireman to hold a hose ejecting water at high velocity?

B) Reaction force pushes hose backward
C) Hose is slippery
D) Fireman is tired

A) Stick breaks the dust particles
B) Inertia of dust resists carpet's motion
C) Wind blows dust away
D) Gravity pulls dust down

B) Inertia can make it fall during sudden motion changes
C) Wind will blow it away

A) Batsman didn't hit hard enough
B) Force of friction opposes the motion
C) Ball naturally wants to stop
D) Gravity pulls it down

A) Mass × acceleration

C) ½ × mass × velocity²
D) Force × time

Q14: According to Newton's first law of motion, an object will continue in its state of rest or uniform motion unless:
A) It gets tired
B) An unbalanced external force acts on it

D) It wants to stop

C) F = m + a

B) For every action, there's equal and opposite reaction

B) It remains the same
